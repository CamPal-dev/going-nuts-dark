REVISIONS

HOW FEEDBACK WORKS.

Every project includes a set number of revision rounds as agreed upfront. We use clear, structured feedback loops so you never feel lost in the process.

  • 01 We share a first cut with notes explaining our creative choices.
  • 02 You give consolidated, specific feedback — not competing opinions.
  • 03 We revise and return with a cleaner version for final sign-off.
  • 04 Final approval and delivery in your chosen formats.
