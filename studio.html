THE STUDIO

WHO WE ARE.

GOING NUTS is a creative video production company based in Los Angeles.

We help brands, corporates, and businesses turn ideas into video content with story, style, and impact.

We are built for clients who want more than someone to show up with a camera. We bring creative direction, production planning, filming, editing, and final delivery together in one flexible process.

Our job is to make the work easier, sharper, and more useful.

Our brand is loud. Your video is built around yours.
