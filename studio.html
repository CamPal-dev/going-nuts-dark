THE STUDIO

WHO WE ARE.

GOING NUTS is a creative video production company based in Los Angeles, California. We create story-led video content for brands, corporates, and businesses across LA and Southern California.

We exist for one reason: strong storytelling and high-quality video shouldn't only be available to giant brands with giant agency budgets. We think like an agency, execute like a production house, and shoot like filmmakers — all in one lean service.

We're built for clients who want more than someone showing up with a camera. We bring creative direction, production planning, filming, editing, and final delivery together in one flexible process — and we help you figure out what to say, how to say it, and how to make it work on screen.

Our job is to make the work easier, sharper, and more useful.

Our brand is loud. Your video is built around yours.
