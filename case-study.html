THE CHALLENGE

THE BRIEF.

Crunch Co. wanted to reintroduce their flagship snack range to a younger audience — but without losing the brand equity they'd spent years building. The brief: make something bold that feels current, stays on-brand, and works as both a TV spot and social content.

The challenge was balancing the legacy brand identity with a tone that didn't feel like it was trying too hard. It needed energy, but it also needed heart.

OUR THINKING

THE IDEA.

We built the concept around a single insight: bold choices aren't loud, they're deliberate. The spot follows a series of real moments — not flashy, not over-produced — where everyday people make choices that feel right. Crunch Co. becomes the thread that connects them.

The visual language was intentionally grounded: handheld energy, natural light, real locations in Los Angeles. We wanted it to feel like something captured, not constructed.

HOW WE DID IT

THE APPROACH.

From creative direction through to final delivery, we handled the full production. Two-day shoot with a crew of 8. Three locations across Los Angeles. 14 total selects captured.

Post-production included offline and online edit, colour grade, licensed music supervision, and three cut-down variants for social distribution.
