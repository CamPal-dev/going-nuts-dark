YOU ASKED

QUICK ANSWERS.

The stuff people always want to know about working with us.

Every project is scoped differently — pricing varies based on complexity, length, crew size, and deliverables. We work across a wide range, from focused single-deliverable projects to large-scale multi-day productions. Tell us about your project and we'll put together a clear, itemised quote with no surprises.

Short-form social content: 2–4 weeks. Standard brand videos and commercials: 4–8 weeks. Large-scale campaigns: 8–12+ weeks. We agree on a timeline before work begins and keep you updated throughout.

Three things: a brief (what you want to make, who it's for, what the goal is), a clear point of contact, and an agreed timeline. We don't need everything figured out upfront — we can help shape the brief together from rough direction.

Rush jobs are possible depending on our schedule. Tight timelines may affect scope, complexity, or revision rounds. Rush fees may apply. Contact us as early as possible — the sooner we know, the more we can do. We'll always be straight about what's achievable.
