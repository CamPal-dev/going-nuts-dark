WHAT AFFECTS PRICING?

⏱
SCOPE & VOLUME

SHOOT COMPLEXITY

THIRD-PARTY COSTS

Talent, props, wardrobe, locations, permits, music licensing, and specialty rentals are quoted separately — always itemized, never hidden.

TIMELINE

Rush jobs and tight deadlines can affect scope and fees.

Our monthly reels packages start at $2,000/month for 4 branded reels, and larger packages get a lower cost per video thanks to production efficiency. Pricing includes concept, strategy, scripting, shooting, editing, and thumbnails — everything except third-party costs like talent or locations, which are always itemized separately.

Video ads start at $2,500, and multi-ad packages come at better rates per ad. That includes target identification, competitor analysis, concept development, storyboarding, production, and editing — the full creative and production process, not just the shoot.

Third-party production expenses: talent (actors, models, extras), props, wardrobe, travel, location fees, permits, stock footage, music licensing, and specialty equipment rentals. We list these separately in every proposal so you always know exactly what you're paying for. No padding, no mystery line items.
